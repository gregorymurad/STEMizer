Bem-Vindo ao STEMizer

Integrando STEM na vida das pessoas

An online educational tool to apply Robotics concepts into STEM fields

As Robotics grows rapidly with the advent of big data and the Internet of Things (IoT), it is necessary for people to study it as early in their education as possible. For example, it becomes essential to understand how sensors that are mounted on a vehicle process thousands of data points each second; how autonomous vehicle do not hit obstacles as the move; how information collected by a robot is read on a mobile or web application in user-friendly manner. Therefore, it is imperative that Robotics becomes part of the K-12 curriculum, and STEMizer is a way to incorporate Robotics and applications into the curriculum in hands-on and creative way, enticing students to ask questions, enhancing creativity and promoting a By the end of high schools, students will be able to create four-legged robots that can help change the world with virtual reality data mapping and even more.

This adaptive curriculum also introduces students to multiple areas, such as machine learning, artificial intelligence, data science, design and engineering

All the modules were developed following the school's curricula so the students will never get lost when mathematical concepts are required; instead, students will reinforce their math knowledge through challenging and fun exercises.

If needed, STEMizer is also flexible enough to be adapted to the context of each school. Teachers have to ability to move modules back and forth to ensure quality instruction that meets the educational requirements and standards.

  • It is fully adaptable to the school's educational structure and policies
  • Interactive design that promotes collaboration and teamwork
  • Engaging environment for learning and doing experiments
  • Exercises adapted to several modern robotic platform (VEX, Arduino, Raspberry PI and Sphero)
  • Long distance access to one of the most technological educational robotics laboratory so students can send their codes and watch in real time the robot executing the commands
